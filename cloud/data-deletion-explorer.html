Data Deletion

How to remove your data from Graphene Cloud Explorer by Graphene Lab

Request Deletion via Email

Out-of-App

If you no longer have access to the app, or have already logged out or uninstalled it, you can request account and data deletion by contacting us directly.

Send an email to privacy@graphene-lab.org with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 30 days. The following data will be permanently deleted: account credentials, stored files, encrypted metadata, and session tokens. No data is retained after deletion is complete.

Manage & Delete Files

In-App
  1. Open Graphene Cloud Explorer on your device.
  2. Long-press to select files or folders you wish to remove.
  3. Tap the Delete icon (Trash bin).
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
Deleted immediately: Selected files and their encrypted metadata are permanently removed from the server.
Retained: Other files in your account remain untouched until manually deleted or the account is closed.
