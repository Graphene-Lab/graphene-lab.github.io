Data Deletion

How to remove your data from Graphene Photos by Graphene Lab

Request Deletion via Email

Out-of-App

If you no longer have access to the app, or have already logged out or uninstalled it, you can request account and data deletion by contacting us directly.

Send an email to privacy@graphene-lab.org with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 30 days. The following data will be permanently deleted: account credentials, synced photos, encrypted metadata, and session tokens. No data is retained after deletion is complete.

Logout

In-App
  1. Open Graphene Photos on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap "Logout".
Deleted from device: cached photos, session tokens, and local account data.
Retained on server: your account and synced photos remain intact until you delete your account.

Delete Account

Permanent
  1. Open Graphene Photos on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the permanent deletion when prompted.
This action is irreversible.
Deleted immediately: account credentials, synced photos, encrypted metadata, and all server-side data.
Deleted from device: local cache, session tokens, and account data.
Retained: none — all data is permanently removed with no retention period.

Clear Synced Photos

In-App
  1. Open Graphene Photos on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap "Clear Synced Photos".
  4. Confirm the action when prompted.
This removes all synced photos from your local device only. Your photos remain safely stored on the server.
