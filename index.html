Who we are

In a digital landscape where cyber threats manifest in ever more insidious forms, from mass surveillance to state-sponsored spyware like Pegasus, Predator, or Reign, encrypting communications alone is no longer sufficient to guarantee privacy and operational safety. It is from this awareness that Graphene Lab was founded: a foundation dedicated to redefining cybersecurity standards for organizations operating in high-risk environments. Our mission is to provide military-grade enterprise solutions for privacy protection and communication security, meeting the most stringent requirements of embassies, governmental bodies, NGOs, and journalists in critical scenarios.

Philosophy

The core of our philosophy rests on the conviction that a compromised device renders any effort to protect data in transit futile. Therefore, our solutions are built upon GrapheneOS, a hardened mobile operating system that currently represents the state of the art in device-level security and privacy. We go beyond merely encrypting calls or messages by making the device itself inaccessible to even the most feared spyware, thanks to rigorous kernel hardening and compiler-based mitigations. This holistic approach ensures that personnel on critical missions can operate with the certainty that their audio and video conversations, along with their sensitive data, are secure from both digital and physical interception or compromise.

Cloud Infrastructure

In the realm of cloud infrastructure, Graphene Lab develops cutting-edge solutions based on Zero-Knowledge Proofs. This means our clients' data, while stored in the cloud, remains a secret accessible only to them, excluding everyone else, including our own platform. This level of security aligns perfectly with European data protection directives and the pursuit of digital sovereignty, offering unprecedented transparency and control for enterprises and institutions that must manage classified or highly sensitive information.

Objectives & Impact

Graphene Lab's objectives are in perfect synergy with the European Union's strategy for resilience. We actively contribute to building a more secure and sovereign digital ecosystem by providing the tools to protect critical infrastructures, counter hybrid threats, and safeguard democratic processes. Through our research and development of advanced encryption and device protection technologies, we stand as a bastion for privacy and an enabler of free and secure communication, fundamental pillars for the security and strategic autonomy of Europe and its partners.
